Our Mission

Bridging Worlds, Inspiring Trainers - The Positive Impact of Pokémon GO

Promoting Active Lifestyle: Studies have shown that Pokémon GO encourages physical activity, with players walking significant distances to catch Pokémon and hatch eggs.

Bringing people together: The game fosters social interaction, bringing people together in parks, neighborhoods, and during special events.

Promoting landmarks: Pokémon GO has revitalized local businesses and landmarks, with many locations becoming popular PokéStops and Gyms.

Improving mental health: The game has a positive impact on mental health, providing a sense of accomplishment, community, and connection with the natural world.
