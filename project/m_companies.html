Our Mother Companies

A Powerful Partnership - Meet the Masterminds Behind Pokémon GO

Niantic: Founded in 2010, Niantic is a leading developer of AR games. Their expertise in location-based experiences and real-world exploration forms the foundation of Pokémon GO's gameplay.

Niantic's Key Innovations: The Niantic Real World Platform, Lightship AR Developer Kit, Planet-scale AR infrastructure.

Nintendo: Founded in 1889, Nintendo is a legendary video game company responsible for creating Pokémon in 1996. Their dedication to quality and storytelling has captivated fans for generations.

Nintendo's Contributions to Pokémon GO: The entire Pokémon universe, including characters, storylines, and game mechanics.
