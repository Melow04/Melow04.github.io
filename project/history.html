Our History

A Timeline of Innovation and Exploration (2016 - Present)

2016: Pokémon GO launches, igniting a global phenomenon with its groundbreaking AR gameplay. Players around the world explore their surroundings, catching and training Pokémon for the first time in the real world.

2017: Gyms and Raids are introduced, encouraging collaboration and teamwork among Trainers. Legendary Pokémon make their debut, raising the stakes and excitement.

2018: Trading and battling between friends become available, fostering a stronger sense of community within the game. The first Community Day event takes place, offering exclusive encounters and rewards for a limited time.

2019: Team GO Rocket arrives, challenging Trainers to battle Shadow Pokémon and free them from their clutches. Research tasks are introduced, providing players with new goals and objectives to complete.

2020-Present: The world continues to expand with new generations of Pokémon, special events celebrating holidays and anniversaries, and ongoing updates that keep the gameplay fresh and engaging.
