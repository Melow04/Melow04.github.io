About Us

Our History
Pokémon GO took the world by storm in 2016, becoming a global phenomenon that revolutionized mobile gaming. This innovative augmented reality (AR) experience brought the thrill of catching and training Pokémon to the real world for the first time. Click to see more about our journey and how Pokémon GO continues to evolve!
Our Team
We're a passionate team of FEU Tech students dedicated to reimagining the Pokémon GO website with a fresh perspective. Our goal is to remake the main website of Pokémon GO using the web development techniques we've mastered in our IT0043 course under the guidance of Sir Joseph Calleja. Click to see more information about our team.
Our Mother Companies
Pokémon GO is a collaboration between two industry leaders: Niantic and Nintendo. Niantic, pioneers in AR gaming, developed the technology that brings Pokémon to life in your neighborhood. Nintendo, the creators of Pokémon, has brought these cherished characters to generations of fans. Click to see more about our amazing partnership!
